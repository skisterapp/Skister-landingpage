Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what data Skister collects, how we use it, where it is stored, and your rights under GDPR.

1. Data we collect

  • Account data: name and email address.
  • Gear listings: item details you provide (photos, descriptions, condition).
  • Rental activity: requests, handoffs, returns, and related timestamps.

2. How we use data

  • To operate the app and provide its core features (inventory, rentals, reminders).
  • To maintain safety and trust features (condition confirmation, ratings within your network).
  • To improve features and reliability (aggregated insights and troubleshooting).

3. Storage & third-party services

Skister stores and processes app data via Supabase (a third-party infrastructure provider). Our Supabase project is configured to use an EU region. Your data may be processed and stored on Supabase-managed services as needed to operate Skister.

We may use additional third-party services (e.g., error monitoring) in the future. If we do, this policy will be updated.

4. Data retention

We store your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide Skister’s services.

When you request deletion, we delete or anonymize personal data unless we are legally required to keep certain records for a longer period.

5. Sharing

We do not sell your data.

  • Your gear and activity details are shared only within your chosen network as part of the app’s normal operation.
  • We disclose data if required by law, or to protect users and the service from abuse.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

  • Right of access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to deletion
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object

To request access, correction, or deletion, contact us at privacy@skister.app.

7. Children

Skister is not intended for users under 16. If you believe a user under 16 has provided personal data, contact us at privacy@skister.app.

8. Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@skister.app

Support: support@skister.app
